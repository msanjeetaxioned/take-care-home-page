Watch the TakeCare Films

The TakeCare films exemplify how you can care for and transform your mind, body, spirit and community. Award-winning, independent filmmakers bring us true stories of amazing individuals and communities who embody the TakeCare spirit. We invite you to watch and be inspired.

  • Drawn to the Mat

    Yoga is for everyone. After learning yoga changes one woman's life, she brings its transformative power to her community of working-class African Americans. Together, they realize the power of mind, body, spirit and community.

  • Splash

    A team of women playing competitive basketball in their 80s and 90s: a mind-body-spirit-community approach to aging.

The TakeCare Campaign

The TakeCare Campaign wants to inspire you to re-imagine your health through powerful story telling of inspiration and transformation. TakeCare of your whole health - mind, body, spirit and community - so you can live life fully for whatever matters most to you. Learn more about the campaign, its films, resources, events, and sponsors, and let us know if you'd like to get involved.
